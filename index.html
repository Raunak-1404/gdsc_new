Our Mission

Our mission involves community engagement,
leadership development, building strong tech
foundation, while enabling all tech enthusiasts to
contribute to the global society.

What Keeps Us Going?

Our club helps students to connect, learn, empower and
grow. Teamwork, innovative thinking, communication,
and leading with solutions is what helps us achieve new
heights.
The entire team works in coordination, to inspire and
motivate the upcoming coding community to evolve
their skills and broaden their horizons of knowledge.

FAQs
What is GDSC?

Google Developer Student Clubs (GDSC) are
community groups for college and university students
interested in Google developer technologies. Students
from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an
interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By
joining a GDSC, students grow their knowledge in a
peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions
for local businesses and their community.
